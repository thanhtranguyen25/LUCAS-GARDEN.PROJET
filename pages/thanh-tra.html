About Me

I am a passionate landscape design student from Vietnam, currently pursuing my Master's degree in Sustainable Landscape Design at Université Côte d'Azur in Nice, France. My academic journey focuses on integrating ecological principles with aesthetic design to create gardens that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible.

Growing up surrounded by the lush greenery of Vietnam, I developed a deep appreciation for the harmonious relationship between humans and nature. This connection inspired me to pursue a career where I could help others experience the joy and tranquility that well-designed green spaces provide.

During my internship at Lucas Gardens, I am expanding my practical knowledge of Mediterranean garden design, sustainable planting techniques, and water-efficient landscaping – skills that I hope to apply in diverse climates around the world.
