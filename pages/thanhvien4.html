About Me

I am an urban landscape architecture student from Barcelona, currently working towards my Master's degree in Urban Ecology and Sustainable Design at Universidad de Barcelona. My research and practice focus on creating community garden spaces that promote social interaction, urban biodiversity, and sustainable food systems.

Growing up in the bustling city of Barcelona, I developed a passion for bringing nature back into urban environments. I've always been fascinated by how green spaces can transform cityscapes and improve the quality of life for residents, particularly in dense urban areas where access to nature is limited.

During my internship at Lucas Gardens, I am exploring the integration of Mediterranean native plants into urban garden designs, implementing community engagement strategies, and learning advanced techniques for sustainable urban landscaping that can be applied in various European cities.
