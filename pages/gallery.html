Featured Project

Luxury Estate Garden Transformation

This comprehensive garden renovation project for a luxury estate in Versailles involved a complete redesign of the 2-acre property. The client wanted a garden that balanced formal French garden traditions with modern sustainable practices.

Location Versailles, France
Completion June 2024
Project Size 2 Acres
Duration 4 Months

The project included formal garden areas, water features, a kitchen garden, and extensive perennial plantings. Sustainable elements include rainwater harvesting, native plant selections, and solar-powered lighting.
