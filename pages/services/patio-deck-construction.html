Our Construction Process

We follow a comprehensive, step-by-step process to ensure your patio or deck is built to the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

1. Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. We discuss how you plan to use the space, your style preferences, and any specific features you'd like to incorporate.

2. Site Assessment

Our team evaluates your property, taking measurements and assessing factors such as topography, drainage, sun exposure, and existing structures to inform the design and construction approach.

3. Design & Planning

Based on our consultation and site assessment, we create detailed designs for your approval, including layout, dimensions, materials, and features. We also secure any necessary permits for the project.

4. Proposal & Contract

We provide a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, materials, timeline, and cost. Once approved, we finalize the contract and schedule your project for construction.

5. Construction

Our skilled craftsmen build your patio or deck according to the approved design, ensuring proper foundation, structural integrity, and attention to detail at every step of the construction process.

6. Final Inspection

Upon completion, we conduct a thorough inspection with you to ensure everything meets our high standards and your expectations. We provide care instructions to help maintain your new outdoor space.

Material Options

We offer a variety of high-quality materials for patios and decks, each with unique benefits and aesthetic qualities.

Wood Decking

Traditional and timeless, wood decking offers natural beauty and warmth. We work with various wood species including cedar, pine, and exotic hardwoods, each selected for their durability and appearance in your environment.

Composite Decking

Low-maintenance and eco-friendly, composite decking combines wood fibers and recycled plastics for exceptional durability and resistance to weathering, insects, and fading, with minimal maintenance requirements.

Natural Stone

Elegant and enduring, natural stone patios create a timeless look that ages beautifully. Options include limestone, sandstone, granite, and slate, each offering unique colors and textures for your outdoor space.

Concrete Pavers

Versatile and cost-effective, concrete pavers come in countless shapes, sizes, colors, and textures, allowing for creative patterns and designs while providing excellent durability and value.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a patio or deck?

The timeline for patio and deck construction varies depending on the size, complexity, and materials used. A simple deck or patio might take 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects with multiple features could take 3-4 weeks or longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Do I need a permit to build a deck or patio?

Permit requirements vary depending on your location and the scope of the project. In many areas, decks attached to the house or above a certain height require permits, while ground-level patios might not. Our team handles all necessary permit applications and ensures your project meets local building codes.

What's the most durable decking material?

Composite decking generally offers the best durability and lowest maintenance, with a lifespan of 25-30 years. Natural hardwoods like ipe and teak are also highly durable but require more maintenance. The best choice depends on your budget, aesthetic preferences, and local climate conditions.

How much maintenance do patios and decks require?

Maintenance requirements vary by material. Wood decks typically need cleaning and resealing every 1-2 years. Composite decking requires only occasional cleaning. Stone patios may need periodic resealing and occasional weed removal between joints. We provide detailed care instructions for your specific installation.
