At Lucas Gardens, we specialize in designing and installing custom outdoor lighting systems that transform your landscape after sunset. Our expert lighting designers create personalized solutions that highlight your property's best features while providing practical illumination.

From subtle path lighting and accent spotlights to dramatic feature lighting and functional security systems, we craft lighting designs that complement your landscape and architectural style. We use only high-quality, energy-efficient fixtures and advanced control systems.

Our Lighting Services

We offer a comprehensive range of outdoor lighting solutions to meet your aesthetic, functional, and security needs.

Landscape Lighting

Highlight trees, plants, and garden features with well-placed spotlights, uplights, and wash lights that create depth and drama in your nighttime landscape. Showcase your garden's beauty after dark with thoughtfully positioned illumination.

Path & Step Lighting

Enhance safety and navigation through your garden with subtle, glare-free illumination for walkways, steps, and transitions. These lights combine functionality with aesthetic appeal, guiding movement through your outdoor spaces.

Water Feature Lighting

Create magical effects with specialized underwater and perimeter lighting for ponds, fountains, and water features. These lights accentuate the movement and reflective qualities of water for stunning nighttime displays.

Patio & Deck Lighting

Extend the usability of your outdoor living spaces with ambient, task, and accent lighting that creates the perfect atmosphere for entertaining, dining, or relaxing. These lights transform your outdoor rooms into welcoming spaces after sunset.

Security Lighting

Enhance property safety with strategically placed floodlights, motion sensors, and perimeter lighting that deters unwanted visitors and illuminates potential hazards. These systems provide peace of mind while complementing your overall lighting design.

Architectural Lighting

Showcase your home's unique architectural elements with façade lighting, soffit lights, and feature highlighting that adds dimension and character to your property. These techniques reveal the beauty of your home's structure after dark.

Our Design & Installation Process

We follow a comprehensive approach to ensure your outdoor lighting system perfectly complements your property and meets your unique needs.

Consultation & Design

We begin with an on-site consultation to understand your vision, needs, and budget. Our designers assess your property at different times of day and consider your landscape features, architecture, and how you use your outdoor spaces to create a custom lighting plan.

Nighttime Demonstration

For larger projects, we often provide a nighttime demonstration using temporary lights to show you exactly how the proposed system will transform your property. This allows you to experience the effects and make adjustments before final installation.

Professional Installation

Our experienced technicians carefully install your lighting system with minimal disruption to your landscape. We use proper trenching techniques to conceal wiring, position fixtures precisely, and ensure all components are properly connected and protected.

Maintenance & Support

We offer comprehensive maintenance plans to keep your lighting system functioning beautifully year after year. Our services include bulb replacement, fixture cleaning, system adjustments, and repairs to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an outdoor lighting system cost?

The cost of an outdoor lighting system depends on various factors including the size of your property, number of fixtures, system complexity, and the type of fixtures selected. Basic systems typically start around €1,200, while more comprehensive designs for larger properties can range from €3,000 to €10,000 or more. We provide detailed quotes after assessing your specific needs and preferences.

Are LED lights worth the investment?

Yes, absolutely. While LED fixtures may have a higher initial cost than traditional lighting, they provide significant long-term value. LED lights consume up to 80% less energy, last 15-25 times longer (reducing replacement costs), produce minimal heat, and offer superior light quality and control options. They typically pay for themselves in energy savings within a few years.

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies depending on the system's scope and complexity. A basic system might be installed in 1-2 days, while more extensive projects could take 3-5 days or longer. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your property and schedule, and we always clean up thoroughly after installation is complete.

What maintenance do lighting systems require?

Even the best lighting systems require some maintenance to perform optimally. This typically includes periodic cleaning of fixtures, bulb replacement when needed, adjustment of fixtures that may have shifted, trimming of plant growth around lights, and checking connections and transformers. We offer maintenance plans that include regular check-ups to ensure your system continues to perform beautifully.
