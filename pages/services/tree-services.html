Frequently Asked Questions

How often should trees be pruned?

Pruning frequency depends on the tree species, age, and health. Generally, mature trees benefit from pruning every 3-5 years, while younger trees may need more frequent pruning to establish proper structure. Fruit trees typically require annual pruning. Our arborists can assess your specific trees and recommend an appropriate schedule.

How do I know if a tree needs to be removed?

Signs that may indicate a tree needs removal include significant trunk damage, large dead branches, leaning, root damage, or fungal growth around the base. If your tree is showing these symptoms, it's best to have a certified arborist evaluate it. They can determine if the tree poses a safety risk or is beyond saving.

What is the best time of year for tree pruning?

Late winter to early spring (before new growth begins) is generally the ideal time for most tree pruning. However, this can vary by species. Dead, damaged, or hazardous limbs can be removed at any time of year. Our arborists can advise on the optimal timing for your specific trees.

Are your tree services insured?

Yes, Lucas Gardens carries comprehensive insurance coverage for all our tree services. This includes liability insurance and workers' compensation. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request for your peace of mind.
