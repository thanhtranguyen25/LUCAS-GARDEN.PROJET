Our Landscaping Services

We offer a comprehensive range of landscaping services to transform your outdoor space:

Terrain Modeling

We reshape your land to create optimal drainage, beautiful contours, and the perfect foundation for your landscape design. Our terrain modeling services include grading, leveling, and creating berms or swales.

Water Features

Add tranquility and visual interest to your landscape with our custom water features. We design and install ponds, fountains, waterfalls, and streams that create a soothing atmosphere in your garden.

Hardscaping

Our hardscaping services include installing patios, walkways, retaining walls, steps, and other structural elements that provide both function and beauty to your outdoor space.

Planting & Softscaping

We carefully select and install trees, shrubs, perennials, and annuals that thrive in your specific conditions. Our planting plans consider color, texture, seasonal interest, and maintenance requirements.

Landscape Lighting

Extend the enjoyment of your outdoor space into the evening with our landscape lighting solutions. We design lighting schemes that enhance safety, security, and the beauty of your landscape.

Irrigation Systems

Our efficient irrigation systems ensure your landscape receives the right amount of water while conserving this precious resource. We design and install drip irrigation, sprinkler systems, and smart controllers.

Our Landscaping Process

We follow a comprehensive approach to ensure your landscape project is completed to the highest standards:

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough discussion of your vision, requirements, and budget. Our team will visit your property to assess the site conditions and potential.

2

Concept & Design

Based on our consultation, we develop a conceptual design and detailed plan for your landscape. This includes layout plans, material selections, and planting schemes.

3

Proposal & Approval

We provide a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, materials, timeline, and costs. Once approved, we finalize the design and prepare for implementation.

4

Site Preparation

We prepare the site by clearing, grading, and addressing any drainage issues. This crucial step creates the foundation for your new landscape.

5

Installation

Our skilled team executes the plan with precision and attention to detail. We install hardscaping elements, irrigation systems, lighting, and plants according to the design specifications.

6

Final Walkthrough & Maintenance Plan

We conduct a thorough walkthrough of the completed project and provide a detailed maintenance plan to ensure your landscape thrives for years to come.

How long does a landscaping project take to complete?

The timeline for landscaping projects depends on their scope and complexity. Small projects might be completed in 1-2 weeks, medium-sized projects typically take 3-6 weeks, and large-scale transformations can take 8-12 weeks or longer. Weather conditions, material availability, and project complexity all influence the timeline. We'll provide you with a detailed schedule during the proposal phase.

Do you offer warranties on your landscaping work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Plants typically come with a one-year warranty (subject to proper care), hardscaping elements like patios and retaining walls have a 5-year warranty on workmanship, and irrigation systems are covered for 2 years. Detailed warranty information is provided in your service contract.

Can you work with my existing landscape features?

Absolutely! We often incorporate existing trees, plants, structures, and other features into new landscape designs when they're healthy and align with the overall vision. Our initial site assessment will identify elements worth preserving, and we'll discuss options for integrating them into your new landscape.
