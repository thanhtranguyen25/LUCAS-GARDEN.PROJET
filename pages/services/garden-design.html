Custom garden designs that reflect your style and enhance your property's natural features

Creating Your Dream Garden

At Lucas Gardens, we believe that a well-designed garden is an extension of your home and a reflection of your personality. Our garden design service creates custom outdoor spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional and sustainable.

Our team of experienced garden designers works closely with you to understand your vision, lifestyle, and requirements. We consider the unique characteristics of your property, including soil conditions, light exposure, and existing features to create a garden that thrives in its environment.

Whether you're looking for a tranquil retreat, a vibrant entertaining space, or a productive kitchen garden, we have the expertise to bring your garden dreams to life.

Our Design Process

We follow a comprehensive, collaborative process to ensure your garden design meets your expectations and thrives in its environment.

1. Initial Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. This is where we discuss your style preferences, how you plan to use the space, and any specific plants or features you'd like to include.

2. Site Analysis

Our designers conduct a thorough site analysis, assessing soil conditions, sun exposure, drainage, existing vegetation, and architectural context. This helps us understand the opportunities and constraints of your property.

3. Concept Design

Based on our consultation and site analysis, we develop concept designs that outline the overall layout, key features, and planting themes. These initial designs help visualize the potential of your space.

4. Detailed Design

After your feedback on the concept, we create detailed design plans including planting schemes, material selections, and construction details. These comprehensive plans serve as the blueprint for implementing your garden design.

5. Implementation

Our skilled landscaping team brings the design to life with meticulous attention to detail. We handle all aspects of implementation, from hardscaping and soil preparation to planting and finishing touches.

6. Aftercare

We provide comprehensive care instructions and offer ongoing maintenance services to ensure your garden thrives. Our relationship with clients continues long after the initial installation is complete.

Garden Design Styles

We create gardens in a variety of styles to match your preferences and property. Here are some of the design styles we specialize in:

Contemporary Gardens

Clean lines, architectural planting, and modern materials create a stylish outdoor space that complements contemporary architecture. These gardens often feature bold geometric designs and a carefully curated plant palette.

French Formal Gardens

Symmetrical layouts, manicured hedges, and classical features create an elegant and ordered garden with a distinctly French aesthetic. These gardens celebrate geometry, perspective, and the controlled beauty of nature.

Cottage Gardens

Abundant, informal planting creates a romantic and nostalgic garden filled with flowers, herbs, and traditional materials. These gardens have a relaxed, effortless charm that evolves beautifully through the seasons.

Mediterranean Gardens

Drought-tolerant plants, terracotta, and gravel create a sun-drenched garden inspired by the Mediterranean region. These gardens are particularly well-suited to sunny, exposed sites and offer year-round interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a garden design cost?

Garden design costs vary depending on the size and complexity of your project. Our design fees typically start from €500 for small gardens, with more extensive designs for larger properties ranging from €1,500 to €5,000. We provide a detailed quote after the initial consultation.

How long does the garden design process take?

The design process typically takes 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to final design presentation. This timeframe allows for thorough site analysis, concept development, client feedback, and detailed design work. Implementation timelines vary based on the project scope.

Can you work with existing features in my garden?

Absolutely! We often incorporate existing trees, structures, and features into new designs. Our site analysis identifies elements worth preserving, and our designs work harmoniously with these features while transforming the overall space.

Do you offer garden design without implementation services?

Yes, we offer stand-alone garden design services if you prefer to implement the design yourself or work with another contractor. Our detailed design plans include all the information needed for successful implementation.
