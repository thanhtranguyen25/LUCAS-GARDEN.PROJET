Creating a beautiful garden is just the beginning. Maintaining it properly is essential to ensure it thrives and continues to enhance your property throughout the changing seasons.

At Lucas Gardens, our comprehensive maintenance services are designed to take the worry out of garden care. Our team of skilled horticulturists and gardeners provides regular, meticulous attention to keep your outdoor space healthy, vibrant, and looking its best year-round.

Whether you have a newly installed garden or an established landscape, we offer customized maintenance plans tailored to your garden's specific needs and your personal preferences.

Our Maintenance Services

We offer comprehensive garden maintenance services tailored to the unique needs of your outdoor space:

Seasonal Cleanup

We provide thorough spring and fall cleanups to prepare your garden for the growing season and winter months. Our services include leaf removal, debris clearing, bed preparation, and general tidying of your outdoor space.

Pruning & Trimming

Expert pruning of trees, shrubs, and ornamental plants to maintain health, shape, and encourage optimal growth. We use proper techniques and tools to ensure beautiful results without damaging your precious plants.

Lawn Care

Comprehensive lawn maintenance including mowing, edging, aeration, dethatching, fertilization, and overseeding. We keep your lawn lush, green, and healthy throughout the growing season.

Pest & Disease Management

Monitoring and treatment of plant diseases and pest infestations using an integrated pest management approach. We prioritize environmentally friendly solutions to protect both your garden and the ecosystem.

Mulching & Soil Care

Application of quality mulch to enhance soil health, retain moisture, suppress weeds, and improve the appearance of your garden beds. We also provide soil testing and amendment services to optimize plant growth.

Irrigation Maintenance

Regular inspection, adjustment, and repair of irrigation systems to ensure efficient watering. We also offer seasonal startup and winterization services to protect your system year-round.

Why Professional Maintenance Matters

At Lucas Gardens, our maintenance team combines horticultural knowledge with artistic sensitivity to nurture your garden throughout its life cycle. We understand the specific needs of different plants, the challenges of each season, and the unique characteristics of your particular landscape.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my garden be maintained?

The frequency of garden maintenance depends on several factors including the type of garden, its size, the plants it contains, and your desired level of care. Most residential gardens benefit from maintenance visits every 2-4 weeks during the growing season (spring through fall) and monthly or bi-monthly during winter. Large estates or gardens with intricate designs and specialty plants may require weekly attention. During your consultation, we'll recommend an optimal maintenance schedule for your specific garden.

Can you work with my existing gardener or maintenance team?

Yes, we can collaborate with your existing maintenance providers. Some clients have us perform specialized services like pruning or seasonal care while their regular gardener handles routine maintenance. We're happy to discuss how our services can complement your current arrangements to ensure your garden receives the best possible care.

Do you offer organic or eco-friendly maintenance options?

Absolutely! We prioritize sustainable maintenance practices and offer fully organic programs. Our approach minimizes environmental impact through practices like manual weed removal, organic fertilizers, beneficial insect introduction, and water conservation techniques. We'll discuss your environmental preferences during our consultation and tailor our maintenance approach accordingly.

What if I only need seasonal services rather than ongoing maintenance?

We offer a range of seasonal services that can be scheduled as needed, including spring and fall cleanups, one-time pruning sessions, seasonal planting refreshes, mulch application, and winter preparation. These services can be booked individually without committing to a regular maintenance plan.
