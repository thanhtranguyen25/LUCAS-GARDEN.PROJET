Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

Lucas Gardens primarily serves Paris and the surrounding areas, including the Île-de-France region. We also take on projects in other regions of France depending on the scope. Contact us to find out if we service your location.

How much does a garden design or landscaping project cost?

The cost of a garden design or landscaping project varies depending on the size of your property, the complexity of the design, and the materials used. We provide free consultations and detailed quotes before starting any work. Contact us for a personalized estimate based on your specific requirements.

What can I expect during a consultation?

During an initial consultation, one of our garden designers will visit your property to assess the space, discuss your vision and requirements, and understand your budget. We'll then provide you with design ideas and a detailed quote. The consultation typically takes 1-2 hours, after which we'll follow up with a comprehensive proposal.
