About Lucas Gardens

Creating beautiful outdoor spaces since 2008

Our Story

Lucas Gardens was founded in 2008 by Jean-Paul Lucas, a passionate gardener with a vision to transform outdoor spaces across France. What began as a small landscaping service in Paris has now grown into one of the country's most respected garden design and landscaping companies.

With over 15 years of experience, our team has completed hundreds of projects ranging from intimate residential gardens to expansive commercial landscapes. Our growth has been built on a foundation of quality craftsmanship, creative design, and sustainable practices.

Today, Lucas Gardens continues to be a family-owned business, maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that has been our hallmark since the beginning. We take pride in our long-standing relationships with clients, many of whom have been with us since our early days.

Our Mission & Values

Mission Statement

To create sustainable, beautiful outdoor spaces that enhance our clients' lives and respect the natural environment.

Sustainability

We believe in creating gardens that work in harmony with nature. Our designs incorporate sustainable practices, native plants, and eco-friendly materials to minimize environmental impact and maximize long-term beauty.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of craftsmanship in everything we do. From the initial design concept to the final planting, we pay meticulous attention to detail to ensure exceptional quality in every project.

Innovation

We continually explore new design ideas, materials, and techniques to create unique and innovative outdoor spaces. We balance traditional landscaping principles with fresh, contemporary approaches.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and fairness. We stand behind our work with comprehensive guarantees and are committed to building long-term relationships with our clients based on trust.
