Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions about our landscaping and garden services

What services does Lucas Gardens offer?

Lucas Gardens offers a comprehensive range of landscaping and gardening services including:

  • Garden Design
  • Landscaping Construction
  • Garden Maintenance
  • Tree Services
  • Irrigation Systems Installation
  • Outdoor Lighting
  • Patio and Deck Construction
  • Lawn Care
  • Sustainable Garden Solutions
  • Seasonal Garden Preparation

Whatever your outdoor space needs, our team of experts can help bring your vision to life.

How much does a garden design or landscaping project cost?

The cost of a garden design or landscaping project varies depending on the size of your property, the complexity of the design, and the materials used. We provide free consultations and detailed quotes before starting any work.

Factors that affect pricing include:

  • Size of the area to be landscaped
  • Current condition of the site
  • Type and quantity of plants
  • Hardscaping elements (patios, walkways, etc.)
  • Additional features (water features, lighting, irrigation)
  • Accessibility of the site

Contact us for a personalized estimate based on your specific requirements.

How long does a typical landscaping project take?

The timeline for a landscaping project depends on its scope and complexity. Small projects might take a few days, while larger transformations could take several weeks. Weather conditions can also affect the timeline.

Here's a general guideline:

  • Small garden makeovers: 3-7 days
  • Medium-sized projects with some hardscaping: 2-4 weeks
  • Complete garden transformations: 4-8 weeks
  • Complex projects with extensive features: 8+ weeks

During your consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed project schedule.

Do you offer garden maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive garden maintenance services including regular lawn care, pruning, weeding, fertilizing, pest control, seasonal cleanups, and more. We can create a customized maintenance schedule tailored to your garden's specific needs and your preferences.

Our maintenance packages include:

  • Basic Package: Monthly visits with essential care
  • Standard Package: Bi-weekly visits with comprehensive care
  • Premium Package: Weekly visits with complete garden management
  • Seasonal Services: Spring preparation, summer care, fall cleanup, winter protection

Contact us to discuss the best maintenance plan for your garden.

Do you use sustainable or eco-friendly practices?

Absolutely! Sustainability is at the core of our business. Our eco-friendly practices include:

  • Using organic fertilizers and pest control methods whenever possible
  • Promoting water conservation through efficient irrigation systems
  • Incorporating native plants that support local ecosystems
  • Practicing responsible waste management with composting and recycling
  • Implementing permeable surfaces to reduce runoff
  • Creating habitats for beneficial wildlife
  • Using sustainable materials for hardscaping elements

We focus on creating gardens that thrive with minimal environmental impact.

Can you work with my existing garden design?

Yes, we can enhance or maintain your existing garden design. Our team can assess your current garden and suggest improvements or maintenance strategies to preserve and enhance its beauty.

Services for existing gardens include:

  • Garden refreshing and revitalization
  • Plant replacements and additions
  • Pruning and shaping of existing plants
  • Soil improvement and mulching
  • Upgrading irrigation systems
  • Adding new features while preserving the overall design

We respect your original vision while offering professional expertise to optimize your outdoor space.

Do you offer any guarantees on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with guarantees. Our warranty terms include:

  • Plants: One-year warranty (subject to proper care)
  • Hardscaping (patios, walls, etc.): 5-year warranty on workmanship
  • Irrigation systems: 2-year warranty on parts and labor
  • Lighting systems: 3-year warranty on fixtures and installation

Additionally, we provide care instructions for all installations and are always available to address any concerns or questions that arise after project completion. Detailed warranty information is provided in your service contract.

How do I prepare for a landscaping project?

Before we begin your landscaping project, we recommend these preparations:

  • Clear personal items from the work area
  • Ensure clear access to your property for equipment and materials
  • Mark any underground utilities or features you know about
  • Secure pets away from the work area
  • Inform neighbors about upcoming work
  • Provide access to water and electricity sources
  • Remove any plants you wish to preserve before work begins

Don't worry—we'll provide you with a detailed preparation checklist specific to your project and handle all the heavy lifting and technical preparations.

Can you help me create a low-maintenance garden?

Absolutely! We specialize in designing low-maintenance gardens that stay beautiful with minimal effort. Our strategies include:

  • Selecting drought-tolerant and native plants that thrive with less care
  • Installing efficient, automated irrigation systems
  • Using quality mulch to reduce weeding and water needs
  • Incorporating hardscaping elements to reduce plant areas
  • Implementing proper soil preparation for healthier plants
  • Creating garden zones based on maintenance requirements
  • Selecting perennials instead of annuals where appropriate

We'll work with you to understand your lifestyle and available time for garden care, then design a beautiful space that matches your maintenance preferences.
